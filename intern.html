INTERSHIPS

Our team members collaborated with different professors from top reputed universities in the world via internship programs held in the respective universities and have done lot of achievements.

  • NTU, SINGAPORE: 3 of our team members worked with 3 different professors focussing in VR based application for construction sites and the other two members are working on an intensive CFD based research.
  • INDIAN INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE, INDIA: 2 of our team members worked in two labs one from aerospace department focussing on autonomous navigation of aerial robots and the other working on heterogenous robot collaboration in industrial workspaces under mechanical department.
  • ALBERTA UNIVERSITY, CANADA: 1 of our team member worked on drone control using brain waves for disabled.
  • MCMASTER UNIVERSITY, CANADA: 1 of our team member worked here on soft robotic arm manipulation and control using sinosoidal torque and MPC based controller system.
  • ARIZONA STATE UNIVERSITY, USA: 2 of our team member worked here on 3D reconstruction of construction sites using RGBD cameras in a lab under the same professor.
  • YUAN-ZE UNIVERSITY, TAIWAN: 3 of our team members worked with 3 different professors on semantic segmentation, CFD + machine learning and ROS based KUKA robotic arm control.
  • UNIVERSITY OF SASKATCHEWAN, CANADA: 1 of our team member worked on 3D printable biomaterials like feasible tissue / bone marrows.
  • NATIONAL CHENG KUNG UNIVERSITY, TAIWAN: 1 of our team member worked on instance segmentation for cancer cell recognition with high an IOU of 94.78%.
  • YORK UNIVERSITY, CANADA: 1 of our team member worked on CFD analysis of surge waves.
  • THOMSON RIVER UNIVERSITY, CANADA: 1 of our team member worked here on CFD anlaytical methods for drainage pipes.
  • UNIVERSITY OF QUEBEC-TROIS RIVIERES, CANADA: 1 of our team member worked here on composites.
